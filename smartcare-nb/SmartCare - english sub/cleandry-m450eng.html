CLEANDRY M450

For cleansing of temporary invalids, incontinent senile patients and newborns. Simply moisten the mitt's pre-soaped palm surface and massage the body part to be treated. The soap contains 0.05% max. Chlorhexidine Digluconate used as a preservative, with slight anti-bacterial effect. For a complete bed bath, start at the head, then move on to the back, chest, legs, and feet, and finish with the genital and anal areas. A practical, hygienic, and fast solution. Reduces operating costs because, compared to traditional washing solutions, it saves time and fewer products are required. It avoids the use of multiple-use towels and/or sponges, which must be washed after each use and are a potential source of microbiological contamination. Soft, absorbent and gentle on the skin, it helps to prevent incontinence-related irritations and ensures patient comfort. The product is latex-free. Do not use on wounded skin. In case of contact with eyes or mucous membranes, rinse thoroughly with plenty of water.
